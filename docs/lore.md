# AttributeLoop — World Lore

## Core Narrative: 折纸师的未竟之愿

A mysterious origami master wove this looping world, believing that folding a **perfect loop** would grant their deepest wish. They died before completing it.

The origami creatures left behind endlessly refold themselves in this world — they are **misfolded wishes**, trapped wills that cannot rest.

The player is drawn into this loop and must walk it to completion. Each encounter with an origami creature is not a fight but a **pattern clash** — the player's attribute rules collide with the creature's fold pattern. The stronger pattern refolds the weaker one. When defeated, the creature leaves behind its folding knowledge (component drops) and dissolves into lighter paper fragments.

## Mechanic ↔ Lore Mapping

| Game Element     | Lore Equivalent              |
|------------------|------------------------------|
| Attribute rules  | Fold patterns (折叠模式)      |
| Combat           | Two fold patterns colliding   |
| Component drops  | Absorbed folding knowledge    |
| Loop track       | The origami master's world    |
| Verdict loop     | The final perfect-fold trial  |
